==========================================
VITALSPAN LONG-TERM PRODUCT VISION
Version 1.0
Do Not Deviate
==========================================

Vitalspan is NOT a health tracker.

Vitalspan is NOT an AI chatbot.

Vitalspan is NOT another PubMed reader.

Vitalspan is a premium longevity magazine powered by AI.

Every design and engineering decision should reinforce that identity.

========================================================
PHASE 1 — EDITORIAL MAGAZINE
========================================================

Goal:
Transform Articles into a premium digital magazine.

Reference:

- Apple News+
- Nature Briefing
- NYTimes Science
- Financial Times Weekend
- Monocle
- Wallpaper*
- The New Yorker

The app should feel like opening a beautifully curated weekly publication.

NOT:
- Feed
- Blog
- CRUD list
- AI summaries

========================================================
PHASE 2 — AI EDITORIAL VOICE
========================================================

Highest priority.

Claude must stop behaving like a paper summarizer.

Claude becomes a science editor.

Writing inspiration:

- Nature Briefing
- NYTimes Science
- Economist Science
- STAT News

Every article should naturally answer:

Why this matters

↓

What happened

↓

Why researchers care

↓

What the limitations are

↓

What to watch next

Tone:

- calm
- objective
- elegant
- intelligent
- never sensational
- never clickbait
- never overpromise

Vitalspan must become recognizable by its writing style.

========================================================
PHASE 3 — EDITORIAL ART DIRECTION
========================================================

Every issue receives its own editorial cover artwork.

This is NOT random AI art.

Create one permanent Style Guide.

Every future cover follows that guide.

References:

- The New Yorker
- Nature
- Apple keynote artwork
- Monocle
- Kinfolk

Avoid:

- stock photos
- floating DNA
- blue HUD graphics
- glowing brains
- generic AI art

Goal:

Someone should recognize a Vitalspan cover instantly.

========================================================
PHASE 4 — FULL AUTOMATION
========================================================

Weekly pipeline:

PubMed

↓

Ranking

↓

Claude Editorial

↓

Draft

↓

Editor Review

↓

Publish

↓

Archive

↓

Next Issue

Cron becomes enabled ONLY after the pipeline is completely trusted.

========================================================
PHASE 5 — PREMIUM EXPERIENCE
========================================================

Future ideas:

Audio Brief

Reading Streaks

Apple Watch summaries

========================================================
DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
========================================================

Less information.

Better hierarchy.

More whitespace.

Editorial typography.

Premium photography.

Museum-quality illustrations.

Every screen should feel expensive.

========================================================
NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES
========================================================

Never feel like ChatGPT.

Never feel like PubMed.

Never feel like Google News.

Never become a generic health app.

Always feel like opening the newest issue of a premium science magazine.
